Fionna
A figment of Ice King's imagination.
Release date 19-03-2014
Attack range type Melee
Roles Fighter, Tank

Abilities

Tenacious (Passive)

Fionna does more Attack damage attack damage and Power damage power damage as her Health health drops. The less hp she has, the more damage she does; up to an 80% bonus when at 1% Health health. Tenacious bonus damage does not affect structures (Towers and Bases).

Cooldown: none

Blade Rush (Q)

Fionna dashes forward and slashes enemies. Blade Rush can be used 3 times in succession before going on cooldown. Each dash can be used in adifferent direction; the first two do damage (20 + 40% Power damage power damage) in a small area, while the final slash hits a larger area (20 + 40% Power damage power damage) and slows enemies by 50% for 1 second. If Fionna doesn't hit anything with any of the dashes, her Blade Rush cooldown is reduced by 30%.

Cooldown: 10s (7s at level 10)

Fierce / Fearless (W)

Fionna can toggle between Fierce and Fearless. Fierce slowly drains Fionna's Health health (2% per second, can't go below 1 hp) to boost Move speed move speedand Attack speed attack speed by 20%. Fearless slowly heals (1% per second) Fionna while granting bonus Armor armor and Shields shields +30%.

Cooldown: 5s at all levels

Invincible! (E)

For 6 seconds, Fionna cannot be KO'ed. She can still take damage and suffer other effects but can not go below 1 health.

Cooldown: 90s (60s at level 10)
