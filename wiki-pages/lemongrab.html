Earl of Lemongrab
The unstable ruler of the Earldom of Lemongrab.
Release date 19-03-2014
Attack range type Melee
Roles Tank, Jinxer

Abilities

UNACCEPTABLE! (Passive)

When Lemongrab is hit by an enemy, he gains a level of UNACCEPTABLE!. Each level increases the damage and stun time of DUNGEON! by 10% and decreases the cast delay of the ability (3 max). Lemongrab can only gain a level of UNACCEPTABLE! every 2 seconds and will lose a level after 6 seconds of not being hit by a power.

Cooldown: none

Sound Sword (Q)

Lemongrab's magical Sound Sword emits a cone of pure noise which damages (40 + 30% Power damage power damage) and slows opponents by 40% for 2.5 seconds.

Cooldown: 10s (8s at level 10)

My Juice (W)

Lemongrab drops the Lemon Bomb. After a brief delay (1.25s) a massive lemon drops on the targeted area. Foes caught in the outer ring are damaged (60 + 40% Power damage power damage) and blinded for 4 seconds. Any enemies unlucky enough to get caught in the center ring take damage (60 + 70% Power damage Power Damage) and are blinded for 4 seconds and silenced for 2 seconds. Refuned 30% cooldown if "My Juice" hits only non-champions.

Cooldown: 12s (10s at level 10)

Dungeon! (E)

Lemongrab summons Lemon Dungeon Cells that damages (100 + 30% Power damage power damage) and stuns enemies for 2 seconds in the target area. Damage and duration of DUNGEON! is increased by 10% times Lemongrab's current level of UNACCEPTABLE!. The delay before the ability inflicts damage and stuns is determined by the current level of UNACCEPTABLE!, with a higher level resulting in a shorter delay.

Cooldown: 45s (35s at level 10)
